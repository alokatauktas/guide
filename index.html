Bonuses at the Best Online Sportsbooks

The best online betting sites feature plenty of bonuses that can give your bankroll a boost. Let's discuss these offers and how to maximize your chances of winning with each.

Welcome Bonus (Deposit Match)

Top online betting sites offer a generous welcome bonus, which usually matches your first deposit by a certain amount. You should look for welcome offers upwards of 100% matched up to $1,000.

What to Look for with a Sportsbook Welcome Bonus:
  • Clear wagering requirements that don't give you a headache when reading them
  • 12x rollover or lower
  • Few, if any, market restrictions
  • Reasonable minimum deposit (e.g., $20)
  • Fair expiry date (e.g., 14 days or longer)

No Deposit Bonus

Online betting sites occasionally hand out no-deposit bonuses to new signups and even existing bettors. These offers aren't very large (e.g., $10 free bet), but they can lead to 'free' winnings.

What to Look for with a No Deposit Bonus:
  • Availability – sportsbook no deposit bonuses are rare, so jump on one if you see it
  • The ability to cash out winnings without depositing real money beforehand
  • Light registration requirements when you have to sign up
  • Reasonable rollover (i.e., under 15x)
  • Limited market restrictions

Free Bets

You can pick up plenty of free wagers at the best sportsbooks. Some sports betting sites give free bets away every week and/or through their VIP program.

What to Look for with Free Bets:
  • Expiry dates longer than 1–3 days
  • Minimum odds of at least -200
  • Low rollover on free bet winnings
  • Daily/weekly availability from your favorite online sportsbook

Reload Bonus

Reload bonuses are a promotion that rewards your loyalty with extra funds when you top up. We commonly see reload offers in the 50% up to $100–$250 range. You can grab up to 25% extra on every deposit with a fair 7x rollover.

What to Look for with a Reload Bonus:
  • Low rollover (5x–10x) since these bonuses are often small
  • More reload offers through the VIP program (if available)
  • Limited restrictions on bet types
  • Solid minimum odds in the -200 to -250 range

Odds Boosts

All the best online sportsbooks offer odds boosts to some capacity. They typically enhance odds on the underdog, like boosting +150 to +200.

What to Look for with Odds Boosts:
  • Boosts that actually beat the market
  • Win limits higher than $50
  • Max bets worth more than $25
  • Not restricted to specific bet types (e.g., "only for 4+ leg parlays")
  • Dedicated odds boost sections at the best online sportsbooks

Parlay Insurance

Many top US sportsbooks now offer parlay insurance, refunding part of your stake if one leg loses. These promos typically apply to 4+ leg parlays and are credited as free bets.

What to Look for with Parlay Insurance:
  • Each leg often needs to meet minimum odds (for example, -200 or longer)
  • Some sites only run parlay insurance on major leagues or specific events
  • Refund limits are usually capped between $25 and $50 per bet

Loyalty Bonus

The best betting sites commonly offer loyalty bonuses (e.g., free bets, cash) through their VIP programs. They also give better rewards as you continue placing wagers and increasing your VIP status.

What to Look for with a Loyalty Bonus:
  • Automatic VIP program enrollment so you can earn loyalty bonuses immediately
  • Generous free bets and deposit bonuses that get better at higher tiers
  • Loyalty points that can be traded for rewards of your choosing
  • Unique perks like a VIP host and/or sporting event tickets/packages
  • The best betting sites will offer other rewards like birthday gifts and exclusive contests

Popular Bet Types at the Best Online Sportsbooks

Let's quickly discuss the most popular bets you'll find at the best online sportsbooks.

Moneyline

Most Common

You simply try to pick the winning side. Favorites have negative odds (e.g. -175), while underdogs have positive odds (e.g. +140).

Tampa Bay Rays +140 vs New York Yankees -175

$100 on Yankees (-175) → $157.14 total, $57.14 profit

$100 on Rays (+140) → $240 total, $140 profit

Point Spread

Popular in NFL

Decide whether the favorite or underdog will cover their assigned point spread.

New Orleans Saints -6.5 (-110) vs Atlanta Falcons +6.5 (-110)

The favored Saints must win by 7+ points for a successful bet.

The Falcons can lose by up to 6 points (or win) to cover the spread.

Totals (Over/Under)

Great for NBA

Bet whether the combined score of both teams will be over or under the sportsbook's line.

LA Lakers over 230.5 (-110) vs Sacramento Kings under 230.5 (-110)

Over: teams must score 231+ combined. Under: 230 or less.

Props

High Variety

Wagers on specific outcomes within a game, like player stats or special events, not just who wins.

Which NFL team will score the first touchdown?

(MLB) Tarik Skubal over/under 7.5 strikeouts

(NBA) Domantas Sabonis over/under 13.5 rebounds

Futures

Big Payouts

Wager on distant future outcomes, like who'll win the championship or lead the league in scoring.

Super Bowl Winner

Philadelphia Eagles +650 | Buffalo Bills +700 | Baltimore Ravens +700

Kansas City Chiefs +800 | Detroit Lions +950 | Cincinnati Bengals +1500

Parlays

Multiplied Odds

Two or more outcomes (legs) on the same ticket. You must win every leg and offers big payouts when successful.

3-leg parlay: New York Knicks to win -150 | Steph Curry over 24.5 pts -125 | Bijan Robinson to score a TD +115

$100 bet returns ~$647 total ($547 profit)

Live / In-Play Bet

Real-Time Action

Wager on action in the middle of games, with odds shifting in real time based on what's happening on the field.

Pre-match: New York Jets +165 vs Buffalo Bills -200

Jets go up 10–7 in Q1 → Live line: Jets -135 vs Bills +115

Five Tips for Betting at Online Sportsbooks

The following advice can help boost your chances of success at the best online sportsbooks.

1

Track Live Betting Odds

When in-play betting, track live odds as frequently as possible. This allows you to jump on opportunities with good value and possibly use the cash-out feature to lock in profits.

2

Avoid Overloading Parlays

Parlays can be decent bets when you do your research and keep the number of legs low. But US online betting sites make roughly 5% more vig per leg, so limit your parlays to 2–4 outcomes.

3

Watch Team News & Stats

Consistency is key when you're trying to win. We recommend monitoring team news, stats, and injuries for each bet in your preferred sports leagues and competitions.

4

Understand Bonus Requirements

You'll have an easier time separating good betting bonuses from the bad when you understand T&Cs. For example, 10x rollover gives you a much better chance at cashing out compared to 18x wagering requirements.

5

Stick to a Budget

Figure out how much you can bet on sports each week/month and stick to that amount. The best online sports betting sites make this easier by providing tools for deposit and wagering limits.
