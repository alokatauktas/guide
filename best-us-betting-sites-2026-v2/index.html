Popular Bet Types at US Betting Sites

American sportsbooks offer a wide variety of bet types. Here are the most popular formats you'll encounter.

Moneyline

Most Common

Simply pick the outright winner of the game. Ideal for moneyline bets on baseball and hockey.

Dallas Cowboys -250 vs New York Giants +200

$100 on Giants (+200) → $300 total, $200 profit

$100 on Cowboys (-250) → $140 total, $40 profit

Point Spread

Popular in Football

A handicap is applied to the favorite and underdog to level the field. This is the main bet type for NFL and NBA games.

Dallas Cowboys -6.5 (-110) vs New York Giants +6.5 (-110)

$110 on Cowboys — they must win by 7+ points to cover.

$110 on Giants — they can lose by up to 6 or win outright.

Totals (Over/Under)

Great for Basketball

Bet on whether the combined score of both teams will be over or under the bookmaker's line.

LA Lakers vs Boston Celtics — Game Total Over 224.5 (-110) / Under 224.5 (-110)

Back the over if you expect a fast-paced, high-scoring matchup.

Player Props

High Value

Wager on specific individual player statistics, such as passing yards, points scored, or home runs hit.

Patrick Mahomes to throw Over 2.5 touchdowns: +120

LeBron James to score Over 25.5 points: -115

$100 bet on Mahomes → $220 total if successful

Futures

Big Payouts

Bet on long-term outcomes, such as which team will win the Super Bowl or NBA Championship, before or during the season.

Super Bowl Outright Winner

Kansas City Chiefs +550 | San Francisco 49ers +600 | Baltimore Ravens +800

Detroit Lions +1200 | Philadelphia Eagles +1400 | Buffalo Bills +1600

Parlay

Multiplied Odds

Combine multiple individual wagers into a single ticket. All selections must win for the parlay to payout, offering much higher odds.

3-leg parlay: Chiefs to win -150 | Yankees to win -200 | Celtics to win -180

$20 parlay returns ~$67 total ($47 profit) if all three win

Live Betting

Real-Time Action

Place wagers in real time as the game is being played, with odds updating dynamically after every play.

Pre-match: Dallas Cowboys -7 vs Philadelphia Eagles +7

Eagles score an early touchdown and lead 7-0...

Live Spread: Cowboys -2.5 vs Eagles +2.5 — significant value shift

Is Sports Betting Legal in the US?

Sports betting legality in the United States varies on a state-by-state basis following the Supreme Court's decision to strike down PASPA in 2018. Over 30 states have launched legal, state-regulated sports betting markets.

For states without local regulated apps, or for bettors looking for better promotions and higher limits, internationally licensed sportsbooks (offshore betting sites) offer a legal alternative. These sites operate outside US jurisdiction under international gaming licenses, allowing them to legally accept US players from across the country.

✅ Legal Offshore Use:
Internationally licensed offshore sportsbooks are accessible and accept players from almost all US states without legal issue for individual bettors.
⚠️ Local Regulations:
Certain states limit local operations, but individual US players are not legally restricted from registering and placing wagers on offshore licensed sites.

Five Tips for Betting at US Online Sportsbooks

Whether you are a seasoned bettor or placing your first football wager, these five tips will help you maximize your value at US betting sites.

1

Shop for the Best Line

Line shopping is key. Point spreads can differ by half a point or more across books. Getting the Cowboys at -6 instead of -6.5 can be the difference between winning and pushing.

2

Manage Your Bankroll

Establish a unit size (usually 1-2% of your total bankroll) and stick to it. Never chase losses by raising your stakes on the Sunday Night Football game.

3

Leverage Player Props

Sportsbooks put less analytical focus on secondary player props than main point spreads, making props one of the best areas for bettors to find statistical value.

4

Understand Key NFL Numbers

In football, margins of victory frequently land on key numbers like 3, 7, and 10. Learn to buy points or choose book spreads that take advantage of these patterns.

5

Register Across Multiple Sites

Having active accounts at two or more top US sportsbooks allows you to easily claim reload bonuses and choose the best odds for every single bet.
